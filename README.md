This project forms part of my postgraduate work in Predictive Analytics and focuses on the application of statistical learning methods and data-driven modelling techniques to a complex e-commerce customer dataset.

The dataset contains a diverse range of behavioural and operational attributes, including subscription status, payment preferences, shipping type, discount and promotional usage, purchase frequency, temporal purchase patterns, and regional segments. The primary research objective was to investigate how these factors influence customer purchasing behaviour and to develop predictive models that support evidence-based decision-making.
